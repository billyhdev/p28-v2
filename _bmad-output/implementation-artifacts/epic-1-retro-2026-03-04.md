## 3. Successes and strengths

- **Contract-first and single facade:** `lib/api` contracts and facade (auth, data, realtime) gave a clear boundary; no backend SDK in app/components. Supabase is one adapter; app code stayed stable.
- **Design system and tokens:** Single theme source (`theme/tokens.ts`) and primitives (Button, Card, Input, ListItem, Avatar, Badge) with 44pt targets and WCAG 2.1 AA. Warm & Soft direction applied consistently.
- **Accessibility:** Steady improvement across stories: tab bar, primitives, auth, profile, notifications, language, conduct. `accessibilityLabel` and `accessibilityHint` used on interactive elements.
- **Error handling:** ApiError and `getUserFacingError` used in UI; inline errors and retry/error-state patterns (e.g. notifications) improved UX.
- **i18n and profile:** EN/KO/KM with locale in profile and LocaleContext; language choice persisted and applied app-wide.
- **Verify scripts and tests:** Story-level verify scripts and unit tests (facade, auth adapter, profile, i18n, conduct) caught regressions and kept boundaries (no SDK in app) enforced.

---

## 4. Challenges and growth areas

- **Accessibility and tokens in review:** Several stories needed follow-up for a11y and “no magic numbers”; building both into the definition of done earlier would reduce rework.
- **Race and UX on preferences:** Story 1.6 (notification preferences) had rapid-toggle race and error-state clarity; request-id and “hide toggles + retry” improved robustness and UX.
- **Profile story status:** Story 1.5 file showed “review” while sprint-status had it done; small tracking inconsistency to avoid in future.

---

## 5. Key insights and learnings

1. **Invest in contracts and facade early (1.3).** It made auth, profile, notifications, and i18n straightforward and kept the codebase backend-agnostic.
2. **Theme and primitives first (1.2)** reduced rework in later stories and kept visuals and touch targets consistent.
3. **Code review and verify scripts** (no SDK in app, contract exports, a11y) paid off; continuing both for Epic 2 is recommended.
4. **i18n tied to profile** (preferredLanguage + LocaleContext) gave one source of truth and avoided duplicate or conflicting locale state.
